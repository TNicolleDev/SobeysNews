Vaughan Council Discusses Traffic Near Huntington Road

Local officials weigh in on truck volume around the facility as delivery schedules continue to expand.

Staff Report Filed Today · 3 min read

Increased truck traffic around Huntington Road was raised as a discussion item at a recent local council session, according to public meeting notes.

Nearby residents have reportedly raised concerns about congestion during peak delivery windows, prompting officials to say the matter is under review.

No formal changes to road access or delivery scheduling have been proposed as of this report.
