New Shift Rotation Draws Mixed Reactions on the Floor

Workers describe the adjusted rotation as either a welcome change or an unnecessary shake-up, depending entirely on who you ask.

Staff Report Filed Today · 4 min read

The revised rotation, quietly posted at the start of the month, has become one of the more talked-about changes on the floor in recent memory.

Supporters say the new pattern gives them more predictable weekends. Critics argue it disrupts routines that had worked fine for years.

Management has framed the change as a scheduling efficiency measure. Staff, for now, remain split.
