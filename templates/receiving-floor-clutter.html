Receiving Floor Clutter Sparks Another Round of Complaints

Staff say the backlog near the receiving bays has become a running joke — and, more seriously, a genuine tripping hazard.

Staff Report Filed Today · 4 min read

Pallets stacked two and three deep near the receiving doors have become an unofficial landmark, according to several employees who work that section daily.

The clutter has been the subject of at least two informal complaints filed with facilities in the past month, with workers citing both safety concerns and simple frustration.

A brief cleanup effort last week reportedly cleared some space, though staff say the backlog was back within days.
