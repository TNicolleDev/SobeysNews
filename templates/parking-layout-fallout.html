The repainted lines have shifted more than just cars — reportedly a few long-standing alliances too.

Several longtime spots are gone. New ones have appeared in their place. No one seems to know, or is willing to say, who signed off on the change.

For a parking lot, it has generated an unusual amount of conversation.
