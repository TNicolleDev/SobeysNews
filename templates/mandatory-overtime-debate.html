Mandatory Overtime Continues to Dominate Break-Room Conversations

With extra shifts piling up, opinions on the floor remain sharply divided between those chasing the hours and those dreading them.

Staff Report Filed Today · 4 min read

For some, the extra overtime is a welcome chance to pad a paycheck. For others, it's the single most-discussed frustration in the building right now.

Multiple employees describe the break room as split roughly down the middle, with the debate resurfacing every time a new schedule goes up.

No changes to the current overtime policy have been announced, and management has not commented on the ongoing conversation.
