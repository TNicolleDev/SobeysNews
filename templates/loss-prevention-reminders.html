Loss Prevention Reminders Return After Recent Inventory Concerns

A fresh round of signage and briefings has staff wondering what exactly prompted the refresher.

Staff Report Filed Today · 3 min read

New posters and a short briefing session made the rounds this week, both focused squarely on loss prevention procedures.

Staff say refreshers like this aren't unusual, but the timing — right after a quiet inventory review — has fueled some speculation of its own.

No official reason for the refresher has been given, and management has offered no further comment.
